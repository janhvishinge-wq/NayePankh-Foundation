About Us

NayePankh Foundation is a youth-driven NGO focused on education, skill development and community empowerment. We believe every child deserves access to quality learning and opportunities to achieve their dreams. Our foundation works to bridge educational gaps by providing learning resources, organizing workshops, conducting awareness programs, and encouraging personal development.

At NayePankh Foundation, we are committed to nurturing talent, building confidence, and inspiring hope. We envision a future where every child has access to quality education, meaningful opportunities, and the guidance needed to unlock their full potential. By working closely with communities and changemakers, we aim to create a more inclusive, educated, and empowered society for future generations. 🌱✨
